Spillover Analysis

Spillover analysis, based on the work of Diebold and Yilmaz, measures how shocks in one time series are transmitted to others in a system. It helps quantify the degree of interconnectedness and systemic risk. The table shows the percentage of forecast error variance for one series that comes from shocks to another series.
